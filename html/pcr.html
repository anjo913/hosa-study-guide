1. steps

denaturation
denature template DNA, breaking H bonds
tempearture
94°C
annealing
primers anneal to target sequents, forming H bonds
temperature
~50-60°, specific to primer
extension
add nucleotides to 3’ end
  • reads template strand in 3’-5’
  • writes base pairs 5’-3’

repeated 25-40 times to amplify

  • no amplicon until 3rd cycle
  • PCR product, DNA from 5’ of primer to 5’ of other
  • 2(n-3) copies of target, n=number of cycles
